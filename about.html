About Me

My Background

I am an Adjunct Faculty and a Research Assistant at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Independent University, Bangladesh. I teach "Numerical Methods" to approximately 130 students in four sections while also working on a project involving pelvic bone segmentation and fracture detection using deep learning. I have completed my B.Sc. from the Department of Electrical, Electrical and Communication Engineering at the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Bangladesh. My work primarily concerns with the practical applications of deep learning in areas such as computer vision (CV), robotics and intelligent embedded systems.
