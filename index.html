Mahmudul Haque
A Researcher, Teacher and Learner!

I am an Adjunct Lecturer and a part-time Research Assistant at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Independent University, Bangladesh. I have completed my B.Sc. from the Department of Electrical, Electronic and Communication Engineering at the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Bangladesh. I am currently teaching courses on Electrical Circuit Analysis, Algorithms and Introduction to Computer Systems. Previously I have taught Numerical Methods to approximately 130 students in four sections while also working on a project involving pelvic bone segmentation and fracture detection using deep learning.

Research Interests: Computer Vision, Robotics, and Human-computer Interation.
Other Interests: Music, Travelling, and Programming.
