Mahmudul Haque
A Researcher, Teacher and Learner!

I am currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Computer Science at the University of Calgary, Canada. I have joined the Biometric Technologies Laboratory (BTLab) under the supervision of Dr. Marina Gavrilova. My current research involves computer vision, action recognition and explanability.
Previously, I have completed my B.Sc. from the Department of Electrical, Electronic and Communication Engineering at the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Bangladesh in 2022. I was an Adjunct Lecturer and a part-time Research Assistant at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Independent University, Bangladesh upto June, 2024. In my appointment I have taught courses on Electrical Circuit Analysis, Algorithms, Introduction to Computer Systems and Numerical Methods while also working on a project involving pelvic bone segmentation and fracture detection using deep learning.

Research Interests: Computer Vision, Robotics, and Human-computer Interation.
Other Interests: Music, Travelling, and Programming.
