Research Opportunity

Status: Application Open

I am thrilled to announce an exceptional opportunity for the undergraduate students at IUB who are interested in AI and want to work on projects focused on computer vision. I am looking for motivated and talented individuals who are passionate about computer vision and eager to gain hands-on experience in this rapidly evolving domain.

As part of the team, you will have the chance to work on cutting-edge computer vision projects, leveraging state-of-the-art technologies and algorithms. This is an opportunity to contribute to real-world applications and enhance your skills under the guidance of Dr. Saadia Binte Alam and other experienced international collaborators. Whether your interests lie in activity recognition, image segmentation, deep learning, or any other aspect of computer vision, this is the perfect chance to explore and expand your knowledge.

Benefits:
1. Learn and implement state-of-the-art AI concepts related to deep learning and computer vision.
2. Publication in international conferences and journals.
3. Build your research profile, which will support your higher studies.

Expectations:
1. Passionate about the work, whether you already have a background in AI or are new to the field.
2. Willingness to learn and adapt to new technologies, as AI is a rapidly growing field.
3. Ability to collaborate effectively in a team setting.
